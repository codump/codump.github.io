RADAR (MFD page = FCR)

Symbology CRM / RWS mode:
Cursor with highest and lowest scan altitude.Steerpoint.
Current ping on target, gets darker after ping.SAM, Soft-lock.
STT lock.
MFD buttons
NORM - EXPNORM, Normal zoomed out view. EXP, Expand zoomed in view.
🡙40 - 🡙80 - 🡙160View range, lines on right side of MFD are 25%, 50% and 75% of the range.
A6 - A2 - A1Azimuth (scan volume left-right) of 60°, 20° and 10° for radar.
1B - 2B - 3BBar (scan volume up-down) steps for radar.
CRM / RWS mode, Range While Search. Shows real-time radar pings.
1x TMS upSAM, Situational Awareness Mode. Soft-lock, enemy does not know it's tracked.
2x TMS upSTT, Single Track Target.
TMS downUnlock target.
TMS left, shortRequest IFF, Identification Friend-or-Foe responce.
TMS right, longSwitch to CRM / TWS mode.
DMS downMake page SOI.
DMS leftToggle left MFD pages.
CRM / TWS mode, Track While Scan. Shows prediction of targets flightpath.
1x TMS upMake system target / NO-LOCK
2x TMS upSTT, Single Track Target.
TMS right, shortWith cursor not on any target: Make all targets a system target.
TMS right, shortWith cursor on a target: LOCK closest target OR switch to next target.
TMS downUnlock target / dump.

TARGETING POD (MFD page = TGP)

There is no extra information at this moment.
MFD buttons
TV - WHOT - BHOTTV, television view. WHOT, white hot IR view. BHOT, black hot IR view.
CCRP mode, Continuously Computed Release Point. Needs to be selected as bombing mode on the weapon page.
TMS upPoint track (auto-follow).
2x TMS downCenter zero, swivel TGP to current STP (steerpoint).
TMS leftCycle TV/WHOT/BHOT.
TMS rightArea track (STP) 3x max.

ELECTRONIC COUNTERMEASURE POD

ECM transmit knob.ECM buttons.

- With the ECM pod, store config needs to be set to 3.
- Startup the ECM by pressing all 6 square buttons on the ECM panel. If they are on they should have an orange S.
- Set the transmit knob to 1 (by default it's set to 3, which means it's continues transmitting and everyone knows where you are).
- Blue T means it's transmitting for jamming.
CMS buttons
CMS downAutomatic ECM (green S).
CMS leftChaff and/or flare (depending on program).
CMS rightTurn off automatic jamming.

TACAN - ILS - IFR

Barometeric altimeter.T-ILS (1) ICP button.
SEQ dobber.ENTR button.
M-SEL (0) ICP button.HSI, M button and CRS knob.

- If a TACAN channel doesn't have a band mentioned on the map, you should use X-ray.
- The TACAN has 3 modes, namely: REC = receive, T/R = transmit and receive, A/A TR = air to air transmit and receive. They are shown by the DED when on the TACAN - ILS page.
- For IFR you need to be at 300 feet AGL for every mile out. Don't forget to set the barometeric altimeter correctly. Contact approach and request landing, they will reply with the QFE that you need to set in the barometer.

Cheatsheet true heading(shown in the F10 map) to magnetic heading.
Caucasus: -6° Germany: -4° Syria: -4/5° Persian Gulf: -2° Mariana: -1° Nevada: -12° Normandy(Modern): +1° Normandy(1944 WWII): +11/12°

Air Traffic Control procedures

STARTUP
Pilot: [AIRPORT] TOWER, this is [CALL SIGN] (formation) REQUEST STARTUP (intentions: eg. VFR DEPARTURE TRAINING AREA ALPHA) ATC: -
Pilot: - ATC: [CALL SIGN] (formation) START UP APPROVED, QNH [XXXX] [CS] CONDITIONS
Pilot: QNH (read back) [CALL SIGN] ATC: -

CLEARANCE
Pilot: - ATC: [CALL SIGN] REPORT READY TO COPY ATC CLEARANCE
Pilot: [AIRPORT] TOWER, [CALL SIGN] READY FOR CLEARANCE ATC: -
Pilot: - ATC: [CALL SIGN] (FORMATION) CLEARED FOR VFR DEPARTURE TO [ZONE] EXIT [REPORTING POINT] SQUAWK XXXX WHEN AIRBORNE CONTACT APPROACH CHANNEL 3
Pilot: [READBACK] ATC: -

TAXI
TAXI BEFORE TAKEOFF
Pilot: [AIRPORT] TOWER, [CALL SIGN] (FORMATION, N F-16) (AT PARKING XXX) REQUEST TAXI ATC: -
Pilot: - ATC: [CALL SIGN] (FORMATION) TAXI TO HOLDING POINT RUNWAY XX VIA XY, SQUAWK IN SEQUENCE
Pilot: [READBACK] SQUAWK IN SEQUENCE ATC: -
TAXI AFTER LANDING
Pilot: [CALL SIGN] RUNWAY VACATED ATC: -
Pilot: - ATC: [CALL SIGN] TAXI VIA [A|B|C|D] TO STAND XX
Pilot: VIA X TO STAND Y [CALL SIGN] ATC: -

TAKEOFF
Pilot: [AIRPORT] TOWER, [CALL SIGN] READY FOR DEPARTURE ATC: -
Pilot: - ATC: [CALL SIGN] WIND XXX DEGREES XX KNOTS RUNWAY XX CLEARED FOR TAKEOFF, REPORT AIRBORNE
Pilot: RUNWAY XX CLEARED FOR TAKEOFF [CALL SIGN] ATC: -
Pilot: [CALLSIGN] AIRBORNE ATC: [CALLSIGN] ROGER

DEPARTURE
Pilot: [AIRPORT] APPROACH, [CALLSIGN] VFR DEPARTURE EXIT XXX ATC: [CALLSIGN] ROGER
Pilot: - ATC: [CALL SIGN] LEAVING THE ZONE, REGIONAL QNH XXXX, (MONITOR [AIRPORT] RADAR ON CHANNEL 11)
Pilot: XXXX ([AIRPORT] RADAR CHANNEL 11) [CALL SIGN] ATC: -

ARRIVAL
Pilot: [AIRPORT] APPROACH, [CALL SIGN], (position, altitude) REQUEST LANDING INSTRUCTIONS FOR VISUAL APPROACH ATC: -
Pilot: - ATC: [CALL SIGN] [RUNWAY] QNH XXXX, [CONDITION] REPORT ENTRY [XXX]
Pilot: [RUNWAY] [QNH] REPORTING ENTRY [XXX] [CALL SIGN] ATC: -
Pilot: [CALL SIGN] ENTERING XXX ATC: -
Pilot: - ATC: [CALL SIGN] CONTACT [AIRPORT] TOWER CHANNEL 2
Pilot: TOWER CHANNEL 2 [CALL SIGN] ATC: -

HANDOVER TO TOWER
Pilot: - ATC: [CALL SIGN] WHEN VACATING THE RUNWAY, CONTACT [AIRPORT] TOWER CHANNEL 2
Pilot: [READBACK] ATC: -

LANDING (VFR)
Pilot: [AIRPORT] TOWER [CALL SIGN] (FORMATION) AT ENTRY XXX XXXXFT FOR LANDING / TOUCH AND GO ATC: -
Pilot: - ATC: [CALL SIGN] (FORMATION) JOIN INITIAL [RUNWAY NUMBER] (LEFT HAND) BREAK QNH XXX
Pilot: QNH XXXX [CALL SIGN] ATC: -
Pilot: [CALL SIGN] (FORMATION) INITIAL RUNWAY XX FOR LANDING ATC: -
Pilot: - ATC: [CALL SIGN] (FORMATION) BREAK [number of how many people are in front] WIND XXX DEGREES XX KNOTS
Pilot: BREAK [NUMBER] [CALL SIGN] ATC: -
Pilot: [CALL SIGN] ON THE BREAK ATC: -
Pilot: - ATC: [CALL SIGN] FINAL [NUMBER]
Pilot: FINAL [NUMBER], [CALL SIGN] ATC: -
Pilot: [CALL SIGN] FINAL GEAR DOWN TO LAND ATC: -
Pilot: - ATC: [CALL SIGN] RUNWAY XX CLEARED TO LAND
Pilot: RUNWAY XX CLEARED TO LAND [CALL SIGN] ATC: -
