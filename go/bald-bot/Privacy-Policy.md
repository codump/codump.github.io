# Privacy Policy for Bald Bot

**Last Updated: April 30, 2026**

Your privacy is a top priority. This policy explains what data we collect and how we keep it "aerodynamic" and secure.

## 1. Data We Collect
To function correctly, Bald Bot collects and stores the following information:
*   **Discord User ID:** To identify you and link your servers to your account.
*   **Pterodactyl Panel URL & Server IDs:** To know which panel and servers to communicate with.
*   **API Keys:** Required to execute actions (Start, Stop, ConLog) on your Pterodactyl servers.

## 2. Security and Encryption
We take your data security seriously:
*   **Encryption at Rest:** All Pterodactyl API keys are stored using **AES-256 encryption**.
*   **Encryption in Transit:** Data transmitted between the Bot, Discord, and your Pterodactyl panel is encrypted via HTTPS/WSS.
*   **No Sharing:** We never sell or share your data with third parties. Your API keys are used strictly for your requested server actions.

## 3. How We Use Your Data
Your data is used exclusively to:
*   Link your Discord account to your Pterodactyl servers.
*   Fetch console logs (`/conlog`) and server status.
*   Execute power actions (Start, Stop, Restart) at your request.

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion
*   **Right to Delete:** You can delete your stored data at any time by removing your server within the Bot using the appropriate commands.
*   **Full Wipe:** If you wish to have all your data permanently removed from our database, please contact the developer via our support channels.

## 5. Third-Party Services
Bald Bot interacts with the Discord API and the Pterodactyl Panel URL you provide. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of your hosting provider or Discord.

## 6. Contact
If you have questions about your data or security, please visit our [Support Discord](https://codump.github.io/go/discord/) or use the contact buttons provided in the `/guide` command.
